The Future of AI Development

We're creating the tools future software developers will use to build intelligent machines.
Right now it is very difficult to work with AI. You need to first become a good coder, and then learn AI techniques. Modern AI appears to be converging on conceptually simple components, where most of the time spent is on experimenting with different architectures. The architectures are only going to become more complex, making collaboration and scaling a big issue.
Nurture is an IDE that solves these problems, by introducing a modular, intuitive visual language for building AI pipelines. No coding knowledge is required, unless you want to build your own components, dramatically lowering the barrier of entry. Nurture incorporates version control into projects, not just for the architecture designs, but also for the module states, allowing for easy experimentation and analysis of training. Components can be shared, and combined like lego blocks, to create more complex systems that are greater than the sum of their parts. Imagine...
No Coding Necessary
